How this works.

01 · Cross-Verification

Multiple AI systems (Claude, Grok, Gemini, Perplexity) run parallel searches. Findings only enter the record when independent sources converge. Every claim carries a version tag.

02 · Unbiased but Absolute in Logic

No political party is genuine 100% of the time. All political statements are treated as potentially disingenuous until primary sources confirm otherwise.

03 · Every Justification Gets a Counter-Test

For each government claim, the strongest possible defence is identified and stress-tested against evidence. Legitimate grievances are acknowledged before proposed solutions are examined.

04 · Primary Sources First

CIHI, StatsCan, Elections Alberta, Hansard, court filings, peer-reviewed research. Investigative journalism as secondary. Social media only for context, never as sole source.
